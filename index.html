What is SHE?

The SHE (Scheme For Her Empowerment) program is dedicated to empowering female students to realize their full potential as leaders in technical fields. Recognizing the untapped potential within every young woman, SHE is committed to providing a platform that nurtures growth, confidence, and innovation. Our mission is to equip girls with the skills, knowledge, and support necessary to thrive in technical fields and beyond.

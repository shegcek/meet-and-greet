What is SHE?

The SHE (Scheme For Her Empowerment) program is dedicated to empowering female students to realize their full potential as leaders in technical fields. Recognizing the untapped potential within every young woman, SHE is committed to providing a platform that nurtures growth, confidence, and innovation. Our mission is to equip girls with the skills, knowledge, and support necessary to thrive in technical fields and beyond.




Why Attend?


Learn

Attendees at SHE's residential state-level camps delve into diverse health topics through immersive educational experiences, fostering a comprehensive understanding of crucial well-being issues.



Engage

Participants actively engage in interactive workshops and collaborative activities, honing essential skills such as effective communication, leadership, and teamwork while forging connections with peers and mentors passionate about health education.


Explore

SHE's camps emphasize experiential learning, providing hands-on opportunities to apply newfound knowledge and skills in real-world scenarios, promoting empowerment, confidence, and a balanced lifestyle through recreational activities and social events.

Camp Guidelines :

  • 1) All participants must provide a consent letter signed by their parent or guardian.

  • 2) Participants from other colleges are required to obtain an endorsement letter from their principal affirming their approval for attending the camp.

  • 3) If extra accommodations are needed before or after the camp dates, a separate accommodation letter should be obtained from the principal for participants from other colleges.

  • 4) All participants must carry their college ID card.

  • 5) Participants are advised to bring necessary personal items such as medicines, bedsheets, and toiletries (soap, toothpaste, etc.).
